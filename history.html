Your data stays on your device

Workout history is stored in your browser's local storage only. It is never sent to any server, never shared, and not accessible to anyone else — not even to the app's author.

To keep a permanent copy: use Export CSV regularly and save the file somewhere safe (Notes, Files, email to yourself). Each person who opens this app has their own separate history.

Note: clearing cookies, site data, or browser history will permanently delete this history — localStorage goes with them. The CSV export is your only backup.
